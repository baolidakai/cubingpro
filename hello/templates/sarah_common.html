Sledgehammer/Hedgeslammer Notation

The S(Sledgehammer)/H(Hedgeslammer) is not a standard notation method, it's a jargon widely used for Skewb.

Majority of Skewb algorithms could be represented with a combination of S and H.

FCN Notation

Let's next learn the standard FCN (fixed corner notation).

FCN is the notation used for WCA scrambling and major timer apps. Familiarize with yourself this notation so you may easily understand many discussions or example solves.

Similarly to most puzzles, first letter indicates which corner on Skewb to turn; second optional apostrophe indicates whether to turn counter-clockwise.

Always start with white at top, green at front, red at right.
